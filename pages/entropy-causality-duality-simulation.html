Time as a Boundary

This visualization represents a conceptual space. The orange/yellow boundary is the "Present Moment," dividing the "Past" from the "Future." Blue points are "Events." In the paper's view, time emerges from the movement of this boundary and the observation of events crossing it. The past is defined by events the boundary has passed; the future by events it hasn't.

**Drag the boundary horizontally** to simulate the passage of time and see how events move from the future to the past.
